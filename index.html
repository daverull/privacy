Last updated: July 21, 2026

Times Tables・Multiplication does not collect any personal data. None.

The app is designed for children, and we built it to be completely safe and private.

What we collect

Nothing. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, and does not ask for names, email addresses, location, or any other personal details.

Player profiles and progress (such as which times tables have been practiced) are stored only on your device and never leave it. You can delete this data at any time by removing a profile or deleting the app.

No tracking, no ads, no third parties

The app contains no advertising, no analytics, no trackers, and no third-party SDKs. Nothing about your child's activity is measured or sent anywhere.

Works offline

The app works fully offline and does not require an internet connection. It does not make network requests to transmit personal data.

Children's privacy

We take children's privacy seriously. Because the app collects no data at all, it complies with children's privacy regulations including the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including provisions for children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Мы серьёзно относимся к приватности детей. Поскольку приложение вообще не собирает данные, оно соответствует требованиям по защите детских данных, включая COPPA (США) и GDPR (ЕС).
